About EasyTip

EasyTip is a digital tipping platform designed to help wait staff receive tips easily and securely through mobile devices. Our mission is to support those who serve us by providing a seamless tipping experience for customers.

Features

Mobile Friendly

Our platform is optimized for mobile devices, ensuring a smooth and user-friendly experience for both customers and wait staff.

Secure Transactions

We prioritize security with secure payment gateways to protect your transactions and personal information.

Ratings & Reviews

Customers can leave ratings and reviews, helping others choose their favorite restaurants and wait staff.
